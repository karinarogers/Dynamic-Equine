Services

PEMF

PEMF stands for pulse electromagnetic field. This therapy emits electromagnetic waves at different frequencies in order to stimulate and encourage your body’s natural recovery process.

LLIT

LLIT, or low level laser therapy can be used to treat many musculoskeletal pathologies. Laser therapy accelerates healing, decreases inflammation and assits with pain management.

Massage

I practice a variety of massage techniques in order to stimulate blood flow. Improved blood flow brings with it oxygen and nutrients and removes toxins that can build up within the body.

IASTM

Instrument-assisted soft tissue mobilization (IASTM) utilizes specialized instruments to work out soft tissue adhesions, like scars and knots, in your horse’s body.
